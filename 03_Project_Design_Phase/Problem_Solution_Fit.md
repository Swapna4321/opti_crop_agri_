# Problem Solution Fit

## Problem

Farmers face challenges in selecting the most suitable crop due to varying soil conditions, climate changes, and lack of proper agricultural guidance.

## Impact

- Reduced crop productivity
- Financial losses
- Inefficient resource utilization
- Increased farming risks

## Solution

OptiCrop uses Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning to analyze soil and environmental parameters and recommend the most suitable crop.

## Benefits

- Better crop selection
- Improved productivity
- Reduced risks
- Increased profitability
- Sustainable farming practices
