# Customer Journey Map

## Stage 1: Awareness
The farmer becomes aware of OptiCrop through agricultural resources, educational institutions, or online platforms.

### Actions
- Learns about AI-based crop recommendation systems.
- Understands the benefits of smart farming.

### Goal
To find a reliable solution for crop selection.

---

## Stage 2: Exploration
The farmer explores the features and functionality of OptiCrop.

### Actions
- Reviews system features.
- Understands required input parameters.

### Goal
To determine whether the system can help improve productivity.

---

## Stage 3: Data Entry
The farmer enters agricultural data into the system.

### Actions
- Inputs soil nutrient values (N, P, K).
- Enters temperature, humidity, pH, and rainfall data.

### Goal
To receive an accurate crop recommendation.

---

## Stage 4: Analysis and Recommendation
The system processes the input data using machine learning algorithms.

### Actions
- Analyzes soil and environmental conditions.
- Predicts the most suitable crop.

### Goal
To provide accurate and reliable recommendations.

---

## Stage 5: Decision Making
The farmer reviews the recommended crop and makes cultivation decisions.

### Actions
- Evaluates the recommendation.
- Selects the suggested crop for cultivation.

### Goal
To improve crop yield and profitability.

---

## Stage 6: Outcome
The farmer experiences the results of the recommendation.

### Actions
- Cultivates the recommended crop.
- Monitors productivity and yield.

### Goal
To achieve better agricultural outcomes and sustainable farming practices.
